Logistics

We at Tradar Group have a Logistics Management System where we assist you to serve the clients with projects along with distribution, transportation, warehousing*, and Inbound/Outbound.

We are continuously working to expand our logistics scope. We are very intent to provide our customers with nationwide logistics coverage by 2020.

Some services we provide for our clients:

  • Projects: We provide projects in real estate, industrial, infrastructure, highway sectors and many more.
  • Insurance: We provide full insurance during delivery and returns.
  • Shipping: We provide solutions for any shipping requirements at lower costs.
  • Monitoring: We provide tools for monitoring, booking, tracking, and tracing of inventory.
  • Warehouse: We provide warehouses located close to the end consumer for faster delivery*.
