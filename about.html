About Us

  1. Home
  2. About

Tradar Group is one of the leading end to end 3PL logistics specialists in India. We have an enormous number of large heavy duty machinery/vehicles in our disposal. Our presence is there in multiple metro, tier-2, and tier-3 cities. We handle multiple peer to peer logistics operation for our client’s i.e.

  • Transportation of the goods
  • Shipping
  • Quality Assurance
  • Support Service

We also provide services like subletting, Transportation Solutions, and Heavy Vehicle Mobility solutions. We create tailored-made economical and long-term solutions for our clients. With reasonable financing, we’re able to provide multiple types of equipment for construction and industries. We built a respected environment that fully endorse stability, togetherness, and pride in what we do and who we are. We are strongly committed to improving the quality of life of our partners. We aim to become a leader in providing flawless logistics services and effective Supply Chain Management solutions to our clients.
